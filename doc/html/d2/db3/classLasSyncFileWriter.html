◆ intensityCoefficient

double LasSyncFileWriter::intensityCoefficient
protected

Precomputed intensity coefficient.

\[ I_{c} = \frac{65535}{\Delta_{I}} \]

\(65535\) comes from \(2^{16} - 1\) as LAS standard defines 16 bits to store intensity
